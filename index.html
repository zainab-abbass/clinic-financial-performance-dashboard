Project Overview

This executive-level healthcare analytics solution was developed to centralize financial and operational reporting for clinic leadership teams.

The dashboard integrates patient analytics, profitability, department performance, financial reporting, balance sheet monitoring and cash flow management into a single decision-making platform.
