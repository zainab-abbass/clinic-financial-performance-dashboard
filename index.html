Project Overview

Healthcare organizations often struggle with fragmented reporting spread across finance, operations, patient management and departmental performance reporting.

This solution was developed to provide healthcare leadership with a centralized executive reporting platform that combines financial performance, profitability monitoring, patient analytics, operational efficiency, balance sheet visibility and cash flow management into one integrated dashboard suite.

Healthcare organizations often operate with fragmented reporting systems that make it difficult for leadership teams to gain a complete view of financial performance, profitability, patient behavior and operational efficiency.

Fragmented Reporting

Limited Profitability Visibility

Cash Flow Tracking

Department Performance

Finance Transformation Approach

This project was designed as a Finance Transformation initiative rather than a standalone reporting solution. The objective was to integrate financial reporting, operational analytics and executive decision support into a centralized Business Intelligence platform.

Financial Reporting Business Intelligence Executive Dashboards Healthcare Analytics Performance Management Profitability Analysis Cash Flow Monitoring KPI Framework Design Operational Analytics Finance Transformation

Technology Stack

Business Intelligence

Power BI was used as the primary analytics and visualization platform for executive reporting.

Data Transformation

Power Query was utilized for data extraction, cleansing and transformation processes.

Data Modeling

Star schema principles and optimized data relationships were used to improve reporting performance.

DAX Development

Custom DAX measures were developed for KPI calculations, profitability metrics and financial analytics.

Financial Analytics

Financial statements, profitability analysis, ratio analysis and performance monitoring.

Executive Reporting

Designed specifically for leadership teams requiring high-level visibility into business performance.
